The R⬆sing S✹n Camp

This summer camp aims to offer a variety of fun — from outdoor activities to more quieter moments of reflection in a month — for children and teenagers of all ages! Enjoy a more relaxing summer knowing that your children are being cared for with professionalism and enthusiasm!

Below you can see our division of participants into groups with specific activities, carefully adapted and selected for each age group!
Star Group
(5-8)

The Little Ones' Union features more interactive and fun-focused activities, stimulating children's enjoyment and imagination!

── ⋅ ★ ⋅ ──

Activities
- Traditional games
- Finger painting
- Creating adventure journals
- Baking camp cookies
- Treasure hunt

Cloud Group
(9-12)

In this team we will have more radical and interesting practices! With these, we aim to create a spirit of unity in group work, unforgettable memories, and moments of relaxation.
── ⋅ ☁︎ ⋅ ──

Activities
- Natural obstacles circuit
- Talent show
- Night songs and s'mores
- Board games and cards
- Sports picnic

Moon Group
(13-16)

This group of older young people involves more thoughtful and daring tasks, so they never get bored or uninterested in our summer camp!

── ⋅ ⏾ ⋅ ──

Activities
- Weekly outdoor cinema
- Hiking, climbing and rappelling
- Friday themed party
- Rising Sun Olympics
- Mystery box
